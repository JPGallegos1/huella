# Huella

Huella connects WhatsApp conversations from nonprofit teams and external donors to structured operational records. This glossary fixes the project language used in product flows, demos, and implementation discussions.

## Language

**Beneficiario**:
A person or family that can receive help through a campaign.
_Avoid_: necesitado

**Perfil seguro**:
A semi-anonymous representation of a beneficiary or family that can be shown to a padrino without exposing full PII.
_Avoid_: ficha completa, datos personales

**Acompañamiento**:
The human-facing way to present a confirmed padrinazgo in product flows and dashboards.
_Avoid_: match, asignacion

**Padrino**:
An external donor who is assigned to support exactly one beneficiary or family in a campaign.
_Avoid_: donante asignado

**Padrinazgo**:
The active relationship between one padrino and one beneficiary or family within a campaign.
_Avoid_: match, asignacion

**Reserva**:
A temporary padrinazgo held while the donor flow is still being confirmed.
_Avoid_: retencion, celda retenida
